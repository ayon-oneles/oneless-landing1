Data & Account Management

Control your data with the Oneless app by AyonTeam. Here are the clear, simple steps to manage your information or delete your account.

How to Delete Your Account

Requesting the permanent deletion of your Oneless account and all associated data is straightforward. Please follow these steps.

  1. Compose a new email to our support team at ayon.helpdesk25@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  3. Send the email from the address associated with your Oneless account for faster verification.

What happens next?
Once we receive your request, we will permanently delete all of your account data within 30 days. This action is irreversible and will remove your email, authentication credentials, all smoking logs, and behavioral data.

How to Delete Specific Data

The Oneless app is designed to give you direct control over your logged data without needing to delete your entire account.

  1. Open the Oneless app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Analytics Dashboard screen.
  3. Find your "Complete daily history" and select the specific day or entry you wish to remove.
  4. Tap the delete icon to permanently erase that data point.

This action immediately removes the data from your device and is reflected in our systems. It gives you precise control over your personal history.
